The Commercial Pilot Certificate allows you to get paid for flying. Eagle Aircraft Flight Academy provides Part 141 and Part 61 programs to meet FAA requirements efficiently.

## Program Highlights

Students must hold a Private and Instrument rating before starting commercial training. Our programs focus on practical flight experience and exam readiness.

## Key Requirements

- Minimum 190 hours flight time (Part 141)
- IFR rating required
- Class II medical certificate
