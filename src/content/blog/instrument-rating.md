The Instrument Pilot Rating allows a Private Pilot to fly in challenging weather conditions. Eagle Aircraft Flight Academy provides a Part 141 program that includes simulator and in-flight training.

## Program Overview

Students can complete 35 hours of instruction, with up to 14 hours on the full-motion DCX-MAX simulator, gaining experience before flying in actual instrument conditions.

## Key Advantages

- Accelerated Part 141 curriculum
- Training in Cessna 172 G1000 aircraft
- Experienced CFII instructors guiding every step
