Earning your Private Pilot Certificate is the first step toward a career in aviation. Eagle Aircraft Flight Academy offers an FAA-approved accelerated Part 141 program to help you learn efficiently and save money.

## Getting Started

Our Private Pilot program provides structured flight and ground instruction in Cessna 172 and 150 aircraft. Students benefit from our experienced instructors and individualized training plans.

## Key Benefits

- Accelerated training to finish faster
- Affordable tuition and housing options
- Access to the latest Cessna aircraft and simulators
