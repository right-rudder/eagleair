Many student pilots begin training with excitement but fail to finish. The reasons are often preventable.

## Common Reasons Students Quit

- Inconsistent training schedules
- Poor financial planning
- Lack of structure
- Training gaps due to weather or aircraft availability

## The Importance of Momentum

Frequent, structured training leads to better retention, confidence, and lower overall costs.

## Setting Yourself Up for Success

Choosing the right school and training plan can make the difference between quitting and earning your certificates.
