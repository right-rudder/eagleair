Anthony decided to pursue a career in aviation after serving four years in the Air Force. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from the University of Arizona. He trained at Eagle for his Instrument and Commercial ratings while working as a dispatcher. He has over 900 hours and can currently teach Private and Commercial students. In his free time he enjoys watching sports and rooting for Michigan Athletics and the Detroit Lions.
