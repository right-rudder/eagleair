Michael originally started his career at Indiana University with a Bachelors of Informatics and Computer Science. But growing up he always had a passion for aviation, so after graduating and working in the field for a while he decided to pursue his dream. Michael completed all his ratings here at Eagle and has flown Cessna 150s, 172s, Tecnam p2010s, and his family's Piper Cherokee 180s. He has over 600 hours of flight time and can currently teach Private and Commercial students.
